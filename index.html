Hi! I'm Berk Gündüz, a Computer Engineer and mobile application developer based in Ankara, Turkey. I'm passionate about creating mobile apps that are not only functional but also improve people's everyday lives. I primarily work with Flutter, focusing on building modern, fast, and user-friendly applications.

So far, I've developed various projects in areas such as education, personal finance, and task management. I use technologies like Firebase, GetX, Dart, and Flask to build efficient and maintainable applications. I pay close attention not just to how an app works under the hood, but also to how it feels and flows for the user.

I care deeply about design, animation, and interaction. To me, good software isn't just about code—it's about crafting an experience that is intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing. That's why I emphasize UI/UX principles in every product I build.

My goal is to create digital solutions that bring real value to users. I enjoy learning new things, approaching problems creatively, and working collaboratively with others. For me, every project is both a contribution and an opportunity to grow.

Skills

Flutter, Dart, Firebase, GetX, UI/UX, Mobile App Development, Animation, Design
